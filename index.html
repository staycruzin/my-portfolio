About

I am a full-stack software developer based in San Diego, California who focuses on writing clean, elegant, and efficient code. I love to code things from scratch and to watch my ideas come to life in the browser.

When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me somewhere enjoying the great outdoors. I've learned many valuable life lessons in the mountains, and these values — from breaking out of my comfort zone to taking one step at a time — translate from the trail to the workplace. I am constantly seeking out new ways to push the boundaries of my comfort zone and I perceive challenges as an opportunity to learn and grow. Additionally, I have learned to embrace the journey and focus on taking one step at a time so that I can put myself in the best position to achieve goals and deliver consistently through proper planning and by breaking down projects into smaller, manageable parts.

I am passionate about building software that is making a difference in the world and I'm looking to make a big impact at a high growth company w here I can work on interesting projects with positive people. Let's make something special.
