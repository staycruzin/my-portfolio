About

I am a full-stack software developer based in San Diego, California who focuses on writing clean, elegant, and efficient code. I love to code things from scratch and watch my ideas come to life in the browser.


When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me somewhere enjoying the great outdoors. I've learned many valuable life lessons in the mountains, and these values — from breaking out of my comfort zone to taking one step at a time — translate from the trail to the workplace. I am constantly seeking out new ways to push the boundaries of my comfort zone and I perceive challenges as an opportunity to learn and grow. Additionally, by focusing on taking one step at a time, I am able to deliver consistently through proper planning & by breaking down projects into smaller, more manageable parts.


I am passionate about building software that is making a difference in the world and I'm looking to make a big impact at a high growth company where I can work on interesting projects with positive people. Let's make something special.
